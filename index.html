About Me

I’m a creative and motivated teen artist originally from Ukraine, now living in Canada. Art is a big part of my life and how I express myself. I love experimenting, making mistakes, and learning through the process. Creating helps me understand my emotions and the world around me.

I really like animals, especially fish, sheep, and rabbits. Fish feel calm and almost hypnotic to watch. Sheep seem peaceful and comforting, and rabbits are small, soft, and full of personality. I find them inspiring in simple, quiet ways.

There are a few places I really want to visit one day. Japan interests me because of its culture and atmosphere. Switzerland looks unreal with its mountains and nature. Germany attracts me for its history, cities, and creative scene, which I’d love to experience myself.
